Company Background Context

Kolrose Limited is a mid-sized technology consulting and project management firm 
headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria, with approximately 150 employees. The company 
was founded in 2009 and specializes in IT infrastructure, software development, 
and government digital transformation projects across Nigeria.

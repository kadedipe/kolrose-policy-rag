# Kolrose Limited - Business Expenses and Reimbursement Policy

**Document ID:** KOL-FIN-001
**Version:** 2.2
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Department:** Finance
**Approved By:** Chief Financial Officer

## 1. Policy Principles

### 1.1 Guiding Principles
Kolrose Limited is committed to reimbursing employees for legitimate business 
expenses while maintaining fiscal responsibility. All expenses must be:
- **Necessary**: Directly related to business activities
- **Reasonable**: Cost-effective compared to alternatives
- **Authorized**: Pre-approved where required
- **Documented**: Supported by valid receipts and explanations
- **Compliant**: Aligned with tax regulations and client billing requirements

### 1.2 Employee Responsibility
Employees are expected to spend company funds as carefully as their own. 
Extravagant or unnecessary expenses will not be reimbursed and may result in 
disciplinary action.

## 2. Expense Categories

### 2.1 Travel Expenses

#### 2.1.1 Air Travel
- Economy class for flights under 4 hours
- Premium economy for flights 4-8 hours
- Business class requires Director approval (flights over 8 hours only)
- Book at least 14 days in advance for best rates
- Use company-approved travel agency: Swift Travels Nigeria Ltd

#### 2.1.2 Ground Transportation
| Transport Type | Allowance/Policy |
|----------------|------------------|
| Personal Vehicle | ₦350 per kilometer (fuel + maintenance) |
| Ride-Hailing (Uber/Bolt) | Actual cost with receipt |
| Taxi | Maximum ₦5,000 per trip within Abuja |
| Car Rental | Pre-approved for out-of-state travel only |

#### 2.1.3 Accommodation
- Maximum hotel rate: ₦35,000 per night (Abuja/Lagos), ₦25,000 (other cities)
- Airbnb/apartment rentals pre-approved for stays exceeding 5 nights
- Incidentals (laundry, mini-bar) not reimbursable
- Private accommodation with friends/family: No reimbursement

### 2.2 Meal and Entertainment Expenses

#### 2.2.1 Meal Allowances
Per diem meal rates when traveling outside duty station:

| Meal | Maximum Amount |
|------|----------------|
| Breakfast | ₦3,000 |
| Lunch | ₦5,000 |
| Dinner | ₦8,000 |
| Full Day | ₦15,000 total |

Receipts required for meals exceeding ₦5,000.

#### 2.2.2 Client Entertainment
- Pre-approval required for client entertainment exceeding ₦20,000
- Alcohol limited to reasonable consumption with meals
- Entertainment venues must be professional (no nightclubs)
- Maximum of 2 Kolrose employees per client entertainment event

### 2.3 Communication Expenses

#### 2.3.1 Mobile Phone
- Company-issued phone: All business calls/data covered
- Personal phone used for business: ₦10,000 monthly airtime allowance
- International roaming: Pre-approved for business travel only

#### 2.3.2 Internet
- Home internet allowance for remote workers (per Remote Work Policy KOL-HR-005)
- Hotel Wi-Fi: Reimbursable when not included in room rate
- Mobile data top-up during travel: Reimbursable with receipts

### 2.4 Professional Development Expenses

#### 2.4.1 Training and Certifications
- Pre-approved job-related certifications: 100% reimbursement
- Professional association membership: Up to ₦50,000 annually
- Conferences: Registration + travel + accommodation (requires business case)

#### 2.4.2 Educational Assistance
- Relevant degree programs: Up to ₦200,000 per semester (50% upfront, 50% on completion)
- Must be related to current role or career progression path
- Minimum grade of "B" required for reimbursement
- Bond of 12 months post-completion or pro-rated repayment

### 2.5 Office Supplies and Equipment

#### 2.5.1 General Office Supplies
- Procure through Administrative Department when possible
- Personal purchases of supplies: Maximum ₦10,000 without pre-approval
- IT equipment must be purchased by IT Department (standardization)

#### 2.5.2 Home Office Equipment
Remote workers may claim:
- Ergonomic chair: Up to ₦100,000 (once every 3 years)
- External monitor: Up to ₦80,000 (once every 3 years)
- UPS/Stabilizer: Up to ₦40,000 (once every 3 years)
- Items become employee property but remain for company use during employment

## 3. Expense Submission Process

### 3.1 Submission Timeline
- Expenses submitted within 14 calendar days of incurrence
- End-of-month deadline: 5th of following month
- Expenses older than 60 days will not be reimbursed without Director exception

### 3.2 Required Documentation
All expense claims must include:

| Expense Type | Required Documentation |
|-------------|----------------------|
| All Expenses | Completed Expense Report Form |
| Transport | Receipt + mileage log (if personal vehicle) |
| Accommodation | Hotel folio + proof of payment |
| Meals | Itemized receipt (not just credit card slip) |
| Client Entertainment | Receipt + list of attendees + business purpose |
| Supplies | Receipt + business justification |

### 3.3 Approval Authority

| Amount | Approval Required |
|--------|-------------------|
| Up to ₦50,000 | Line Manager |
| ₦50,001 - ₦200,000 | Department Head |
| ₦200,001 - ₦500,000 | Director |
| Above ₦500,000 | Managing Director |

### 3.4 Reimbursement Processing
- Approved expenses processed in next payroll run (25th of each month)
- Urgent reimbursement (within 5 days): Requires Director approval
- Payment via bank transfer to employee's registered salary account only

## 4. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses will NOT be reimbursed:
- Fines, penalties, or traffic violations
- Personal entertainment (movies, recreational activities)
- Lost or stolen personal items
- Expenses for spouses/family members (unless pre-approved)
- Gifts to government officials
- Late fees or interest charges on corporate cards
- Personal grooming or clothing (except branded work attire)
- Expenses without receipts (except per-diem meals within limits)

# Kolrose Limited - Remote Work and Flexible Working Policy

**Document ID:** KOL-HR-005
**Version:** 2.0
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2024
**Department:** Human Resources
**Approved By:** Managing Director

## 1. Policy Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for remote work arrangements at Kolrose Limited, 
recognizing the evolving nature of work in the technology sector while maintaining 
productivity, collaboration, and service delivery standards.

### 1.2 Scope
This policy applies to all full-time permanent employees who have successfully 
completed their probationary period. Contract and probationary employees may be 
eligible on a case-by-case basis with Director approval.

## 2. Remote Work Eligibility

### 2.1 Position Eligibility
Remote work is available to employees whose roles meet the following criteria:

| Eligibility Factor | Requirement |
|-------------------|-------------|
| Task Independence | Minimum 70% of tasks can be performed independently |
| Technology Requirements | Role can be performed with standard laptop and internet |
| Client Interaction | No daily in-person client meeting requirements |
| Supervision Needs | Employee has demonstrated self-management capability |

### 2.2 Employee Eligibility Criteria
Employees must meet ALL of the following:
- Completed probationary period with satisfactory performance rating (3.5/5 or above)
- No active disciplinary warnings
- Reliable high-speed internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps download, 5 Mbps upload)
- Suitable home workspace meeting ergonomic standards
- Successfully completed remote work readiness assessment

### 2.3 Ineligible Circumstances
Remote work will not be approved when:
- Employee is on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
- Role requires access to classified government systems only available on-site
- Employee has been cited for remote work policy violations within the past 6 months
- Project phase requires mandatory on-site presence (e.g., server room installations)

## 3. Remote Work Models

### 3.1 Hybrid Model (Standard)
The standard remote work arrangement at Kolrose Limited is the hybrid model:
- **2 days remote** per week (employees choose Monday/Thursday or Tuesday/Friday)
- **3 days on-site** at Suite 10, Bataiya Plaza, Area 2 Garki, Abuja
- **Core hours**: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM WAT must be worked regardless of location
- **Wednesday is mandatory on-site** day for all staff (team collaboration day)

### 3.2 Fully Remote Model
Full remote work may be approved for:
- Employees residing outside the Federal Capital Territory
- Specialized technical roles with limited local talent pool
- Employees with documented medical conditions
- Director-level approval required, reviewed quarterly

### 3.3 Occasional Remote Work
Employees may work remotely on an ad-hoc basis (up to 5 days per month) for:
- Personal appointments near home
- Minor illness not warranting sick leave
- Inclement weather or security concerns in Abuja
- Line manager approval required via email

## 4. Equipment and Connectivity

### 4.1 Company-Provided Equipment
Remote employees receive:
- Laptop computer with VPN client installed
- Headset for video conferencing
- Security token for multi-factor authentication
- Ergonomic assessment allowance: ₦50,000 one-time payment

### 4.2 Internet Connectivity
- Employee responsible for home internet service
- Monthly connectivity allowance: ₦15,000 for hybrid, ₦25,000 for fully remote
- Must maintain minimum bandwidth standards (10 Mbps down / 5 Mbps up)
- Backup connectivity plan required (mobile hotspot minimum)

### 4.3 Equipment Care and Liability
- Company equipment remains property of Kolrose Limited
- Employee liable for loss or damage due to negligence (excess of ₦100,000)
- Equipment must be returned within 5 working days upon termination
- Personal equipment used for work must meet IT Security standards

## 5. Productivity and Accountability

### 5.1 Work Tracking
Remote employees must:
- Log into the company time tracking system (Toggl Track) during work hours
- Update project management tools (Jira/Asana) with task status by 10:00 AM daily
- Attend all scheduled virtual meetings with camera on (unless bandwidth-constrained)
- Submit weekly accomplishment reports by Friday 4:00 PM

### 5.2 Performance Expectations
Remote work does not alter performance standards:
- All deadlines and deliverables remain unchanged
- Client satisfaction metrics apply equally
- Response time to emails/messages: within 2 hours during core hours
- Available via phone during all working hours

### 5.3 Remote Work Performance Monitoring
Managers will monitor:
- Task completion rates compared to on-site baseline
- Meeting attendance and participation
- Communication responsiveness
- Quarterly performance review scores

Decline in performance exceeding 15% from on-site baseline may result in remote work 
privileges being revoked.

## 6. Communication Protocols

### 6.1 Primary Communication Channels

| Channel | Tool | Response Time Expectation |
|---------|------|--------------------------|
| Instant Messaging | Slack | Within 30 minutes (core hours) |
| Email | Microsoft Outlook | Within 2 hours (working hours) |
| Video Calls | Microsoft Teams | Scheduled in advance |
| Emergency | Phone Call | Immediate |

### 6.2 Meeting Etiquette
- Camera should be on for meetings under 30 minutes
- Professional background (physical or virtual) required
- Mute when not speaking
- Join meetings 2 minutes before scheduled start
- Use "raise hand" feature for orderly discussion

## 7. Remote Work Agreement and Review

### 7.1 Formal Agreement
Approved remote work arrangements require a signed Remote Work Agreement detailing:
- Approved remote work model and schedule
- Equipment provided and allowances
- Performance expectations and monitoring
- Data security obligations
- Review and revocation conditions

### 7.2 Review Process
Remote work arrangements are reviewed:
- **Quarterly**: Informal check-in with line manager
- **Annually**: Formal review and renewal/revocation
- **Ad-hoc**: If performance or conduct issues arise

### 7.3 Revocation of Remote Work Privileges
Remote work may be revoked with 2 weeks' notice for:
- Performance falling below acceptable standards
- Policy violations (e.g., working from unauthorized locations)
- Project requirements changing
- Repeated communication failures

## 1. Company Overview

### 1.1 About Kolrose Limited
Kolrose Limited is a leading technology consulting firm registered under the Companies 
and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) of Nigeria, with registration number RC-9876543. Our 
headquarters is located at Suite 10, Bataiya Plaza, Area 2 Garki, Opposite FCDA, 
Abuja, FCT, Nigeria.

### 1.2 Our Mission
To deliver world-class technology solutions that drive digital transformation across 
Nigeria's public and private sectors while maintaining the highest standards of 
professionalism and integrity.

### 1.3 Our Values
- **Innovation:** We embrace cutting-edge technology and creative problem-solving
- **Integrity:** We uphold ethical standards in all business dealings
- **Excellence:** We strive for the highest quality in every deliverable
- **Collaboration:** We believe in the power of teamwork and partnership
- **National Impact:** We are committed to contributing to Nigeria's digital economy

### 1.4 Company Structure
Kolrose Limited operates through four main divisions:
1. **Technology Infrastructure Division (TID)** - Network design, cloud services, cybersecurity
2. **Software Development Division (SDD)** - Custom software, mobile apps, web platforms
3. **Project Management Office (PMO)** - Government and enterprise project delivery
4. **Corporate Services Division (CSD)** - HR, Finance, Legal, Administration

## 2. Employment Framework

### 2.1 Employment Categories
Kolrose Limited recognizes the following employment categories:

| Category | Description | Benefits Eligibility |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|
| Full-Time Permanent | 40 hours/week, indefinite duration | Full benefits package |
| Contract (Fixed-Term) | Project-based, specified duration | Limited benefits |
| Probationary | First 6 months of employment | Limited benefits |
| Intern (NYSC/IT) | National Youth Service Corps or Industrial Training | Stipend only |

### 2.2 Probation Period
All new employees serve a six-month probationary period. During this time:
- Performance is reviewed monthly by the line manager
- Either party may terminate employment with 2 weeks' notice
- Upon successful completion, employment is confirmed in writing
- Probation may be extended once for up to 3 months

### 2.3 Working Hours
Standard working hours are 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday, with a 
one-hour lunch break from 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM. This constitutes a 40-hour work week.

### 2.4 Location and Remote Work
Primary work location is the corporate headquarters at Suite 10, Bataiya Plaza, 
Area 2 Garki, Abuja. Remote work arrangements are governed by the Remote Work 
Policy (KOL-HR-005).
